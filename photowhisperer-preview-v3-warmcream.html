FAQ

Questions, answered.

Does this replace knowing photography? +
No. It's a co-pilot, not an autopilot. You'll still frame the shot, choose the moment, and click the shutter. PhotoWhisperer just removes the math between you and the picture.
Which cameras does it work with? +
Any camera with manual controls — DSLR, mirrorless, advanced compacts. You tell us your body and lens; we adapt the recommendations to your sensor and aperture range.
What happens when I run out of credits? +
Requests pause until your next billing cycle, or you can top up with extra credits or upgrade your plan. You'll always see your usage in-app before you hit the wall.
Is my data stored? +
Your chats are saved to your account so you can revisit past shoots. You can delete them anytime from your settings. We don't train models on your conversations.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Cancel from the customer portal — your plan stays active until the end of the billing period, then drops to Snapshot.

Scene summary
Sun roughly 30 minutes before sunset, behind subjects under a tree canopy. Expect rim-lit hair, soft front-fill from sky bounce, and a glowing warm background through the leaves. Backlight will lift exposure on faces — meter for the highlights and let shadows fall.
Assumptions
Clear skies · subjects roughly 4–6m apart from background · 85mm wide open is acceptable for soft background.
Warnings
Lens flare likely with sun direct in frame — use a hood or shift angle 15°. If they hug, single AF point on the closer eye.
